John Truckenbrod is the founder of First Advisors®. As a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™, he offers financial advisory services to clients in the areas of investment management, retirement and estate planning. He has been providing financial services in the Twin Cities since graduating from the Carlson School of Management at the University of Minnesota. John spent numerous years in public accounting as a Certified Public Accountant, where he gained extensive business financial and income tax experience.

John is an avid reader, enjoys the outdoors and likes to spend summer weekends boating. He is an advisor to all seeking guidance. John and Jane spend time with their two daughters and at all types of social gatherings.
